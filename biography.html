Hello everybody! My name is Claire Donnan. I am a 19 year old, second-year Digital Journalism major at theUniversity of Ottawa. from St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador, this is my second year living in Ontario.

I've been a lover of performance of all kinds since I was very young. I was born into it, my aunt is a music and theatre teacher, my grandfather is a former actor and classical singer, and my dad has also been singing and acting for much of his life. I started dancing at three, singing and acting at seven, and carried these pastimes through my childhood and even in early adulthood, as I still try to take jobs and roles in the performance sector whenever I can.

When I reached high school and stopped having the time to perform in theatre (though I still trained), I turned my eyes to other creative avenues that would allow me to combine my love of writing and reading with my love of performance. I joined Model UN and the debate team, and discovered a love of writing, arguing and communication. Finally, in grade 12 I restarted my school's newspaper, fell in love with the writing, and the rest was history!

In my day-to-day life as a young adult in Ottawa I've been trying to get back to some of the things I loved when I was younger. I've been looking for theatre and singing projects to take part in, and am getting back into martial arts. Every day is full of new experiences and adventures, and I am so thankful for the life I get to live. Lots of love! -Claire
